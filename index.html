ABOUT US

We are two brothers, Adam and Sean Amadio, from Toronto. After playing dozens of Solitaire games with the same visual style, the same clunky mechanics, and the same overlooked details, we tried to imagine what a Solitaire game could look like in 2013. Inspired by the design direction of iOS 7, we set out to strip the bloat out of one of the most popular games on the planet and recreate the Solitaire experience from the ground up.
